Data Deletion Request

To request that the app developer, Resnick Automotive Group, delete your data, select the Menu, then click into “Profile/Settings”. Click “Edit Profile” then “Delete Profile.

The Following data is what will be removed from the servers:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email
  • Phone Number
  • Vehicle Data
  • Platform (iOS/Android)
  • Loyalty Rewards User Status
  • Any Documents Uploaded to Account

    • Data related to app analytics, such as amount of time spent on the app or which screens were click on, are retained, but they are not associated with any specific user.

    If you have any questions, please contact the dealership.
